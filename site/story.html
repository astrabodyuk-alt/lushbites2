It started with a question.

That question became Lush Bites. And 135 Queen Street became the answer.

02

The technique is everything.

A smash burger is a commitment. You take a ball of fresh beef — never frozen, always 100% British — and you press it hard against a screaming-hot flat iron. The contact caramelises the outside instantly. What you get is those signature lacy, crispy, caramelised edges that no other cooking method can produce.

It's called the Maillard reaction. We call it the reason people come back.

Every patty at Lush Bites is smashed to order. Not pre-pressed. Not resting under a heat lamp. Fresh when you order it.

03

What goes in matters.

100% British beef. Locally baked brioche buns. Fresh vegetables sourced daily. Sauces made in-house — including the Lush Bites special sauce, which is exactly that.

We built the full menu with the same standard. The wings are marinated. The loaded fries use the same sauce. The milkshakes use real Biscoff and Kinder Bueno — not flavouring.

You can taste the difference. That's the point.
