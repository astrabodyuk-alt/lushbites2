Our Story

Born in Portsmouth.
Built on the smash.

Lush Bites isn't a chain. It started right here — at 135 Queen Street — with one obsession: the perfect smash burger. Fresh beef, searingly hot cast iron, and a technique that creates those signature lacy, caramelised edges you can't get any other way.

We've built a full menu around that standard. Every wing, every loaded fry, every milkshake — made with the same care. Portsmouth-made, Portsmouth-proud.
