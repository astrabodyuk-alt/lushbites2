## PROMPT C — Video Transition (Kling 3.0)
*Upload Prompt A image as Start Frame · Upload Prompt B image as End Frame · Duration 5s*

START FRAME: The assembled Lush Bites smash burger — double smashed beef patties with caramelised lacy edges, melted American cheddar, golden brioche bun with sesame seeds, stacked with lettuce, tomato, pickles, and Lush Bites sauce — centred on a near-black #1a1a1a background. Warm fire-red rim light halo. Skin-on fries tumbled to the right. Camera absolutely locked off, zero movement.

END FRAME: The same burger deconstructed — every component (bun crown, lettuce, tomato, cheese, both patties, pickles, sauce ribbon, base bun, scattered fries) floating independently in a vertical explosion arrangement, same near-black background, same matching cinematic lighting, every element suspended and still.

TRANSITION CHOREOGRAPHY — frame-by-frame:

Frames 1–12 (0–0.5s): The burger sits completely still. Perfect silence. The rim light pulses very slightly brighter for 3 frames, building anticipation.

Frame 12 (0.5s): A single compression shockwave radiates outward from the burger centre — a barely-visible pressure distortion ring moving through the air, visible as a subtle atmospheric shimmer. This is the trigger.

Frames 12–30 (0.5–1.25s): BUNS initiate first. The bun crown begins lifting — slowly at first (ease-in), then accelerating through its arc. The bun base simultaneously begins dropping, slower. Both rotate gently as they move. Sesame seeds begin separating individually from the crown, each on its own lazy floating trajectory.

Frames 25–50 (1.0–2.0s): VEGETABLES cascade. The lettuce leaf peels upward with a beautiful unfurling motion. The tomato slice floats free, juice droplets spinning off its edge in a slow-motion orbital spray. Pickles scatter off-axis with a slight spin on each.

Frames 35–60 (1.45–2.5s): CHEESE stretches. The cheddar elongates in a gorgeous melted-cheese pull — the golden drape stretching upward toward the rising top patty while still connected to the lower patty. This stretch is the centrepiece moment. It holds for 12 frames before the connection breaks and the cheese becomes a floating free-form drape.

Frames 45–70 (1.85–2.9s): PATTIES separate. The top patty rises and rotates 12° to display its full caramelised face to camera — the crispy lacy edges catching the rim light spectacularly. The bottom patty descends, exposing its sear-marked underside. Between them, the Lush Bites sauce erupts in a slow amber ribbon arc.

Frames 60–85 (2.5–3.5s): FRIES scatter. Individual fries peel away to the right in a gentle cascade — each one tumbling at its own rotation speed. Salt crystals orbit each fry.

Frames 85–120 (3.5–5.0s): ALL ELEMENTS reach their final floating positions. Motion eases to a full stop. Every ingredient is suspended, perfectly still, in the explosion arrangement. The rim light catches each element independently. The camera holds on this for 1.5 seconds.

MOVEMENT PRINCIPLES:
— All motion is ease-in / ease-out (cubic-bezier, no linear movement)
— Each component moves on a slightly different timing offset — nothing moves simultaneously
— Subtle 3D rotation on every element to reveal volume
— Steam wisps frozen around the patties throughout
— No camera movement whatsoever — locked tripod

STYLE: Photorealistic. Cinematic 24fps. Warm fire-red rim lighting (#e84c1e) maintained throughout entire animation. Near-black background. The energy is premium food ASMR — deeply satisfying, slow, deliberate, tactile. Michelin-star food editorial meets Apple product launch reveal energy.

DURATION: 5 seconds
ASPECT RATIO: 16:9
OUTPUT: Kling 3.0 high-quality mode, maximum resolution
